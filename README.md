# Hello, I am Alule Robert.

I am a **Researcher and Educator of Biological Sciences** based in **Mukono, Uganda**.

Currently, I am pursuing an **MSc in Biochemistry** at **Makerere University** (Department of Biochemistry and Systems Biology), focusing on **Host-Pathogen Interactions** and **Dynamical Modelling**.

### My Mission
I am a results-driven researcher dedicated to creating and improving products for medicine and conservation biology. I combine wet-lab techniques with advanced statistical tools to analyze microbial and pathological data.

### Core Interests
* **Infectious Diseases:** Understanding cellular and molecular mechanisms of disease.
* **Systems Biology:** Applying dynamical modelling to biological systems.
* **Immunometabolism:** Investigating cellular adaptation to stress.
